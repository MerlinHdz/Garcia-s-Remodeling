ABOUT US


The Team at Garcia's Remodeling


Miguel has acquired a knack for selecting some of the best craftsmen in the state to join his team over time. He handpicks them after a grueling process of seeing their talents hard at work. Many are from his hometown in Honduras, others have been referred to him as the place to work.

Miguel helps to teach his crew multiple trades, so if someone is out, the other can pick-up the slack without a hiccup. He takes his time in educating them on how to get it done right the first time, to show-up on time and to do an excellent job, including assessing the situation, deciding on what tools or paint brush to use for that specific job as well as being respectful of the owner’s property and cleaning up when the job is complete.






Miguel Garcia, owner of Garcia's Remodeling




Miguel was born in Honduras, Central America. At a young age, he learned many trades from the townspeople. They would call on him to come and help on various construction projects as the grunt. He quickly learned how to remodel kitchens and bathrooms along with becoming a paint aficionado on what paints to use where as well as laying concrete, bricks and mortar.

In 2006, following his older brother to a better life, he decided to come to America and share his talents with the Austinites. He was hired on with a painting company in Cedar Park as he quickly made footprints in Central Texas.

Within a 2-year period, Miguel decided to go out on his own and open the doors of Garcia’s Remodeling. He developed a clientele within a few months of repeat and referral business. He has done some of the most interesting projects as you will see from the gallery photos and videos from a complete house remodel to the smallest of projects like helping a client change out a door.
